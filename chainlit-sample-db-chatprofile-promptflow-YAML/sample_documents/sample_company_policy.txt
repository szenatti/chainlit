ACME Corporation Employee Handbook

1. INTRODUCTION

Welcome to ACME Corporation! This handbook provides essential information about our company policies, procedures, and benefits. All employees are expected to read and understand these policies.

2. WORK SCHEDULE AND ATTENDANCE

Standard work hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Employees are expected to arrive on time and maintain regular attendance. Flexible work arrangements may be available upon manager approval.

Remote work policy: Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week with prior approval from their direct supervisor. Remote work requests must be submitted at least 48 hours in advance.

3. VACATION AND TIME OFF

All full-time employees accrue vacation time at a rate of 2 weeks per year for the first 5 years of employment. After 5 years, the accrual rate increases to 3 weeks per year. Vacation requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance.

Sick leave: Employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave per year. Unused sick days do not carry over to the next year.

4. BENEFITS

Health Insurance: ACME Corporation provides comprehensive health insurance coverage for all full-time employees. The company pays 80% of the premium, and employees pay 20%.

Retirement Plan: We offer a 401(k) retirement plan with company matching up to 4% of the employee's salary.

Professional Development: The company provides up to $2,000 per year for professional development courses, conferences, and certifications.

5. CODE OF CONDUCT

All employees must maintain the highest standards of professional conduct. Harassment, discrimination, and unethical behavior will not be tolerated. Any violations should be reported to Human Resources immediately.

6. TECHNOLOGY POLICY

Company equipment must be used for business purposes only. Personal use of company computers and phones should be minimal. All data on company devices is subject to monitoring and review.

Employees are prohibited from installing unauthorized software on company computers. Any software installations must be approved by the IT department.

7. CONFIDENTIALITY

All employees must maintain strict confidentiality regarding company information, client data, and proprietary processes. Disclosure of confidential information may result in immediate termination.

8. PERFORMANCE REVIEWS

Performance reviews are conducted annually in January. Employees will receive feedback on their performance and discuss career development goals with their supervisor.

9. TERMINATION POLICY

Employment with ACME Corporation is at-will. Either the employee or the company may terminate employment at any time, with or without cause, and with or without notice.

Upon termination, employees must return all company property, including laptops, phones, access cards, and any confidential documents.

For questions about any of these policies, please contact Human Resources at hr@acmecorp.com or extension 1234.
